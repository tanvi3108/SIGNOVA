About Signova

Bridging hands & words — Sign to Text

Our Vision

Communication is a basic human right — yet millions who rely on American Sign Language (ASL) still face challenges in being understood in everyday interactions. At Signova, we believe that technology should remove barriers, not create them. With compassion and innovation at its core, Signova aims to make communication more inclusive, respectful, and effortless.

What is Signova?

Signova is a smart, lightweight ASL alphabet recognition tool designed to convert single-letter ASL PNG images into readable text. You simply upload an image of a hand sign — and Signova instantly interprets the ASL alphabet gesture and converts it into the corresponding English letter.

Whether you're learning sign language, building assistive applications, or creating inclusive digital experiences, Signova makes the process simple, fast, and accessible.

Why Signova Matters

Many people want to communicate respectfully with deaf and hard-of-hearing communities, but don't always know the alphabet or gestures. Signova bridges that gap by providing:

  • Fast Interpretation: Converts ASL alphabet PNGs to text in seconds.
  • Local Preview: Images stay on your device until you confirm the upload.
  • Simple, Clean UI: Built for clarity, ease of use, and learning.
  • Accessibility-First Design: Minimal, intuitive steps for all skill levels.

Signova isn’t meant to replace real sign language communication. Instead, it empowers beginners, students, teachers, and developers by giving them a reliable tool to recognize and understand ASL alphabet signs quickly.

How It Works

Our system uses a trained machine learning model that reads the shape, orientation, and structure of a single ASL hand gesture from a PNG image. The model then identifies the letter, and the website displays the result instantly in a clean text format. This makes Signova lightweight, accurate, and ideal for academic or real-world use.
